About project

“It’s me” is a photo project about mature women accepting and enjoying beauty of their age conveyed in their natural feminine uniqueness. The beauty of a woman isn’t in her body shape, or the clothes she wears or the way she styles her hair. True beauty comes from within, it is a manifestation of a kind soul whose attractiveness only grows with passing years.

Our project celebrates the natural beauty of mature women, embracing their unique femininity and wisdom gained through life experience.

Main Objectives of the Project

  • Promote to embrace themselves and develop a positive sense of self-worth and assurance

  • Inspire women to embrace their unique beauty at any age

  • Provide inspiration and role models for every women to look up to

  • Encourage women to recognize the beauty with in them selves

  • Highlight the importance of individuality and authenticity in beauty

Our mission

Our project is dedicated to celebrating the beauty of women at any age. We believe that true beauty comes from within and is reflected in our individuality and life experience. Through this photoproject, we want to capture the essence of natural beauty in women of all ages and inspire others to embrace their own unique beauty. Our aim is to break down the societal pressures of beauty standards and empower women to feel confident.
